# **LeadPipe Corporation**

## About Us
LeadPipe Corporation is a small-time Game-Development company based in Sydney, Australia. LeadPipe Corporation (or PbPipe Corporation (or PbP Corp.)) recruits independent developers to create and publish games onto their digital distribution platform called Smoke. So far, PbP Corp. has created several games, including Wormhole, Wormhole 2, Third Life, etc.
